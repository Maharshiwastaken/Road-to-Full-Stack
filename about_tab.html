About

Hi, This is Maharshi.

I welcome you to the about page for my website. This website, it doesn't do anything special. It just is a marker in the world of webs, it is a message that I am here, I exist on the internet.

This was a very hurried project. On a random Friday, I got the motivation to wish this thing into life, I remember staying up until 2 a.m. to build it.

I built this site with elbow grease and duct tape. You can find the source code to this website on my Github profile, and it is frankly embarrassing to look at.

But, it makes for a good story. A story about my website. Getting lost and tangled on so many hurdles, yet, emerging out with an end result. I am proud of my work.
